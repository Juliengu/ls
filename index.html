    Open, Innovative, Agile

    Le studio has been created with the Start Up mindset to fit the needs of all Airbus employees leveraging disruptive innovation and new way of working supporting advanced and improved operation efficiency. Le studio’s missions are:

    Project Acceleration

    Le studio has to accelerate web and mobile projects with digital experts and Startups ecosystem.

    Digital Acculturation

    Le studio has to foster digital acculturation of all employees with an innovative and collaborative place

    Le studio has the agility to boost and accelerate business digital initiatives in order to develop new innovative solutions, new ideas, new customer user experiences, new ways of making operational processes more efficient or reduce business operating costs.
